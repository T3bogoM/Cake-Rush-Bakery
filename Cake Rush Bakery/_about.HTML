Our Story

At Cake Rush Bakery, we believe that every moment becomes sweeter with something delightful. From weddings and birthdays to cozy family gatherings, our treats are crafted to make every celebration memorable.

Established in 2017 by Nomcebo Biyela, a passionate young entrepreneur from the Northern Cape, Cake Rush Bakery began as a small kitchen dream inspired by her grandmother’s timeless recipes. With dedication, creativity, and the power of social media, the bakery grew into a beloved local favorite. To meet rising demand, Nomcebo expanded her business online — allowing customers to browse, customize, and order their favorite cakes with just a few clicks.
