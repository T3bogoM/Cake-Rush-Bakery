Our Story

At Cake Rush Bakery, we believe that every moment is sweeter with something special. We specialise in cupcakes, wedding cakes, and custom orders.

Cake Rush Bakery first opened its doors in 2017, founded by Nomcebo Biyela, a young entrepreneur born and raised in the Northern Cape. Inspired by her grandmother’s baking, she turned her passion into a thriving business that quickly gained popularity through social media.
